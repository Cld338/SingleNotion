# Privacy Policy for Single Notion

**Effective Date: March 2026**

Single Notion ("the Extension", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your data when you use our Chrome Extension. We strictly adhere to the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies, including the requirements for Limited Use.

## 1. Information We Collect and How We Collect It

The Extension does not track your browsing history in the background or collect data continuously. Data extraction occurs **only when you explicitly click the "Capture & Send" (캡처 & 전송) button** in the Extension popup.

* **Data Collected:** The Document Object Model (DOM) data, text, image URLs, page layout, and CSS styles of the currently active Notion page (`notion.so` or `notion.site`).
* **Data Not Collected:** Passwords, payment information, browsing activity on non-Notion websites, and Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as IP addresses.

## 2. Purpose of Data Collection (Single Purpose)

The collected page content is used for one single, explicit purpose: **rendering and converting your Notion page into a PDF document**. 
We do not use your data for marketing, targeted advertising, profiling, or any other unrelated purposes. 

## 3. Data Transmission and Storage

Since the Extension cannot perform PDF conversion locally within the browser, the captured data is securely transmitted to our dedicated server (`https://notion-pdf.cld338.me`) using HTTPS encryption.

* **Temporary Processing:** The transmitted data is held in the server's temporary memory solely for the duration of the conversion process and your active editing session.
* **Automatic Deletion:** Immediately after the session expires or the conversion process is completed, all captured page data and the resulting PDF files are completely and permanently deleted from our server. We do not archive or maintain persistent backups of your Notion content.

## 4. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third parties, including advertisers or marketing partners. The data is processed exclusively within the secure cloud infrastructure directly managed by us for the sole purpose of providing the PDF conversion service.

## 5. Required Permissions

To provide its core functionality, the Extension requests the following permissions in its `manifest.json`:
* **Host Permissions (`https://*.notion.so/*`, `https://*.notion.site/*`):** Required to access and capture the HTML/CSS content of your active Notion pages for PDF conversion. The Extension cannot and will not access content on any other websites.

## 6. Your Rights and Control

You maintain full control over your data. You can disable, uninstall, or remove the Single Notion extension at any time through the Chrome Extension Manager (`chrome://extensions/`). Uninstalling the Extension immediately revokes all of its access and permissions.
